# TalentDrop Web v1 — Site Architecture

## Strategic Thesis

TalentDrop is a Sports Decision Infrastructure.

The athlete is the origin.

Talent Passport is the core product.

Evidence, Readiness, Certificate and Decision are generated from the Talent Passport.

B2C, B2B and B2G are different ways of consuming the same athlete-centered infrastructure.
