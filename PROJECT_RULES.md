# TalentDrop Web v1 - Project Rules

## Strategic Principles

The athlete is always the origin.

Talent Passport is always the core product.

Evidence, Readiness, Certificate and Decision are built on top of Talent Passport.

B2C, B2B and B2G are consumption layers, never the origin.

## Communication Rules

Do not position TalentDrop as:

* Scouting platform
* Marketplace
* Social network
* Athlete database
* ERP
* Generic SaaS

Position TalentDrop as:

Sports Decision Infrastructure.

## Product Hierarchy

Athlete
→ Talent Passport
→ Evidence
→ Readiness
→ Certificate
→ Decision

## Design Rules

White background.

Navy blue primary color.

Light blue accents.

Minimalist enterprise style.

Apple + Stripe + Notion + Vercel inspiration.

No visual clutter.

## Development Rules

One task = one change.

Do not redesign without explicit instruction.

Do not modify sections outside the requested scope.

Always preserve strategic hierarchy.
